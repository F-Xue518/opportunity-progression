# AWS Sales Stages (AWOS) — Exit Criteria → MEDDPICC Mapping

Source: GCR Field Enablement — MEDDPICC Card & AWS Sales Stage Framework

## Stage 1: Prospect
**Exit Criteria:**
- Customer opportunity identified
- Opportunity has products with valid estimated value (non-zero dollar)
- Initial customer meeting scheduled

**MEDDPICC Element Alignment:**
- I (Implicate the Pain): Identify potential pain/needs; customer's compelling event
- E (Economic Buyer): Initial budget direction
- M (Metrics): Establish evidence-based opportunity value estimate
- DP (Decision Process): Initiate engagement process; who implements strategic solution
- CH (Champion): Assess if contact could become Champion

## Stage 2: Qualified
**Exit Criteria:**
- Customer is willing to pursue a solution
- Customer champion & decision-maker identified
- Delivery partner strategy identified

**MEDDPICC Element Alignment:**
- I (Implicate the Pain): Validated pain; buyer's desired business outcomes
- M (Metrics): Initial business success metrics defined
- CH (Champion): Customer internal supporter confirmed
- DP (Decision Process): Budget and timeline known
- E (Economic Buyer): Ultimate decision-maker identified
- CO (Competition): Competitive strategy determined

## Stage 3: Technical Validation
**Exit Criteria:**
- Customer and AWS share common understanding of solution architecture, implementation, and process
- Technical solution design finalized and agreed with customer

**MEDDPICC Element Alignment:**
- DC (Decision Criteria): Technical requirements agreed
- DP (Decision Process): Implementation process confirmed; technical decision steps and stakeholders
- M (Metrics): Technical success metrics established
- DC (Decision Criteria): Technical success evaluation criteria established; ruled out "do nothing"
- CH (Champion): Technical champion endorsement obtained
- M (Metrics): Technical metrics confirmed

## Stage 4: Business Validation
**Exit Criteria:**
- Relevant customer approvals secured
- Deal details reviewed and agreed with customer on commercial terms and close plan

**MEDDPICC Element Alignment:**
- E (Economic Buyer): EB approval
- DP (Decision Process): Close plan reviewed and close date confirmed; resolve decisions that may affect procurement
- P (Paper Process): Customer procurement steps understood; initial approval documents
- M (Metrics): Financial metrics confirmed
- P (Paper Process): Contract process determined
- E (Economic Buyer): Commercial terms agreed

## Stage 5: Committed
**Exit Criteria:**
- Customer solution started migrating to AWS OR usage reached 10% of opportunity MRR value
- Customer signed agreement (Private Pricing Agreement)

**MEDDPICC Element Alignment:**
- M (Metrics): Migration plan meets business metrics
- DC (Decision Criteria): Deployment timeline clarified
- P (Paper Process): Contract terms negotiated and executed
- E (Economic Buyer): Final EB contract confirmation

## Stage 6: Closed/Launched
**Exit Criteria:**
- Delivery governance implemented
- Opportunity has been won

**MEDDPICC Element Alignment:**
- DP (Decision Process): Project decision process requirements met
- M (Metrics): Post-implementation business metrics met
- DC (Decision Criteria): Project decision criteria met
- P (Paper Process): Delivery-related documents signed
